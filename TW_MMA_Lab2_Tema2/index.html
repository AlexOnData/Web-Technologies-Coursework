Overview

Welcome to the Student Survey micro-site. This page collects feedback from students enrolled in the Web Technologies course at Universitatea Transilvania din Brașov.

Your input helps us improve the lecture content, lab exercises, and the homework difficulty progression. The survey is anonymous and static — nothing is sent anywhere; this is purely an HTML/CSS demo.

Read the FAQ if you have questions about how the survey is structured.
