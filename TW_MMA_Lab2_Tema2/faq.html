Questions

1. Is the survey anonymous?

Yes. The Name and Email fields on the survey form are optional and only used to identify multiple submissions. As this is a static HTML page (Lab 2 demo), no data is actually sent or stored.

Back to top

2. Where is my data stored?

In a real survey, the answers would be stored in a database such as Firebase Firestore (covered in Lab 9). For this demo, the form is purely visual and submission does nothing.

Back to top

3. Can I change answers later?

Use the Reset button to clear the form, or refresh the page. Submissions are not stored, so you can fill the form as many times as you want.

Back to top

4. How long does the survey take?

The survey contains 5 short sections and typically takes 2-3 minutes to complete. We recommend reading the overview first.

Back to top

5. Who has access to the results?

In a real version, only the course instructors and TAs would have access. Aggregated results may be shared anonymously with future students.
